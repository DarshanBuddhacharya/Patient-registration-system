About us

The Dhanvantari
Medical Team

The project is proposed to be a patient registration website of hospitals with a brain tumor and Pneumonia detection. This project aims to minimize the hassle and time taken in the hospital. When you visit a hospital there is a lot of checkpoints that you need to go through. This website plans to eliminate this hassle by making the patient registration online. Users can fill out the registration form in their homes and make an appointment with a concerned doctor by just press of a button.

Our Aim

1. Patient Registration
2. Medical History Display
3. Brain Tumor detection
4. Pneumonia detection
5. Doctor’s note and feedback
6. Appointment booking

Our Objective

1. New patients can register with signup form
2. Returning patients can login to their previous accounts
3. Patient’s symptom with any previous dieses’ registration
4. Pneumonia and brain tumor detection with ML
5. Patients can book appointment with concerned doctor
6. Doctors can view patient’s data (Backend)
7. Doctors can provide feedback and notes to their respective patients
