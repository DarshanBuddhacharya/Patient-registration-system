Welcome to Dhanvantari

Your very own Patient registration system

The project is proposed to be a patient registration website of hospitals with a brain tumor and Pneumonia detection. This project aims to minimize the hassle and time taken in the hospital. When you visit a hospital there is a lot of checkpoints that you need to go through.

This website plans to eliminate this hassle by making the patient registration online. Users can fill out the registration form in their homes and make an appointment with a concerned doctor by just press of a button.

This website features brain tumor detection as well as a Pneumonia detection system with the help of a deep-learning algorithm called convolutional neural network (CNN). The patient can visit diagnostic centers with are comparatively less crowded and get an X-ray/ MRI scan. The scan can be uploaded on the website where the algorithm can detect if the patient is healthy or not. If the result is 3 adverse then the patient can visit the hospital for special treatment.
